Glazed Carrots

Uncategorized

Prep Time: 10 min Cook Time: 15 min Difficulty: Easy Servings: 2 servings (Scaled) Source: Foodnetwork.com

Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound carrots, approximately 7 medium, peeled and cut on the bias 1/4-inch thick
  • 1/2 ounce (2 tablespoons) unsalted butter
  • Heavy pinch kosher salt
  • 1/2 cup good-quality ginger ale
  • 1/4 teaspoon chili powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon chopped fresh parsley leaves

Directions

In a 12-inch saute pan over medium heat, combine the carrots, butter, salt and ginger ale. Cover and bring to a simmer. Once simmering, remove the lid, stir, and reduce the heat to low. Cover again and cook for 5 minutes. Remove the lid, add the chili powder and increase the heat to high. Cook, tossing occasionally, until the ginger ale is reduced to a glaze, approximately 4 to 5 minutes. Pour into a serving dish and sprinkle with the parsley. Serve immediately.
