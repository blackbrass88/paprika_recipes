Lowcountry Hoppin' John

Beans

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 6 to 8 Source: Sean Brock

Ingredients

  • Peas:
  • 2 quarts stock, pork or chicken
  • 1 cup red peas
  • 1 1/2 cups onion, medium diced
  • 1 cup carrots, medium diced
  • 1 1/2 cups celery, medium diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1 bay leaf, fresh
  • 10 thyme sprigs
  • 1/2 jalape√±o, chopped
  • Kosher salt
  • Pea Gravy:
  • Reserved 1 cup cooked red peas
  • Reserved 2 cups cooking liquid from peas
  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • Cider vinegar
  • Garnish:
  • Diced chives or scallions

Directions

Peas:

Bring stock to a simmer.

Add drained peas and all other ingredients.

Cook partially covered over low heat until soft, about 1 hour.

Drain, reserving liquid. Pull 1 cup peas and 2 cups liquid for gravy. Return remaining peas & liquid to pot.

Gravy:

Blend reserved peas, liquid, & butter until smooth, about 3 minutes.

Add cider vinegar to taste.
