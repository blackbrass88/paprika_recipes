Sesame Noodles

Uncategorized

Servings: Yield: 1 dinner and 1 lunch Source: Blog.ruhlman.com

Ingredients

  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 teaspoons chile flakes (or 3 or 4 depending on disposition)
  • vegetable oil as needed, 2 tablespoons or so
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 3 ounces peanut butter (a heaping quarter cup by volume)
  • 1/2 pound pasta, cooked and chilled
  • 8 ounces cooked chicken, chopped or shredded
  • 1/2 pound asparagus, cooked and shocked
  • 1/2 cucumber seeded and cut in strips
  • 3 scallions thinly sliced
  • 2 tablespoons sesame seeds
  • sesame oil to taste

Directions

In a sauté pan over medium high heat, cook the pepper flakes and garlic in the oil until the garlic is tender. Add the lime, sugar, soy, fish sauce, peanut butter. Stir till it’s all combined and the peanut butter has melted and it’s uniform.

Combine pasta and chicken in a bowl and pour the peanut sauce over it, tossing till the pasta is coated. Season to taste with sesame oil and keep combining.

Serve topped with cuke, asparagus, scallion, and sesame seeds.
