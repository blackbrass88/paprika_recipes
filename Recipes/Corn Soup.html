Corn Soup

Soup/Stew

Difficulty: Easy Servings: 4 to 6 Source: Good and Cheap

Ingredients

  • 4 cups corn, fresh, canned, or frozen
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 stalks celery, finely chopped
  • 1 bell pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 small potato, diced
  • 4 cloves garlic, finely chopped
  • 1 chili pepper, finely chopped
  • 1 tbsp cornmeal
  • 5 cups broth, corn or vegetable
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Directions

Melt butter in large pot over medium heat.

Sauté onion, celery, bell pepper, & potato. Cover and let stream for 5 minutes.

Remove lid, add garlic & chili pepper and sauté for 5 mins.

Add the corn & cornmeal. Cover with the broth.

Bring to a boil, then lower heat and simmer for 30 minutes.

Salt & pepper to taste.
