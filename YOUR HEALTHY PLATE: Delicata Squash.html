YOUR HEALTHY PLATE: Delicata Squash

Uncategorized

Source: Yourhealthisonyourplate.com

Ingredients

  • 2 delicata squash
  • 1/4 cup hazelnuts, lightly roasted
  • 1/3 cup pomegranate seeds
  • 1/4 cup pecans
  • 2 Tbsp. dried cranberries
  • 2 Tbsp. extra-virgin olive oil
  • salt & pepper

Directions

Prepare a cookie sheet with foil, and set oven temperature to 350F.

Slice the squashes open lengthwise, remove seeds, and wipe the inside flesh with olive oil from your finger. This will take maybe 1 tsp of the entire amount. Sprinkle with salt & pepper. Place on cookie sheet.

Rub the hazelnuts between your fingers (or shake in a small paper bag) until most of the skins are removed. Chop hazelnuts coarsely with a sharp knife, and distribute evenly on the squash halves.

Sprinkle pecans, cranberries and pomegranate seeds directly onto the squash halves. Sprinkle on remaining olive oil.

Place in oven for approx 25-35 min until flesh yields easily to a fork. Remove and allow to cool for a few minutes. Serves 2.

This makes a magnificent meal with a simple green leaf lettuce salad, dressed only with olive oil and lemon juice. Delicata is a wonderful squash that looks gorgeous and tastes the same. It’s a little bit sweet, and a little bit not. Plus you can eat the skin of the delicata — so enjoy!
